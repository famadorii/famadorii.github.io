about

a chicano digital humanist

I am a PhD Candidate at Stony Brook University who studies and teaches courses on US, Latin American, and environmental history. Born and raised in a suburb of Los Angeles, I attended Chapman University before going to Stony Brook for my graduate work. I am currently finishing my dissertation, "Landscapes of Longing: Migration and the Remaking of Rural Mexico."
